1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

The BSM AI scenario system was tested across 9 positions with 13 total scenario
attempts via the "AI Coach's Challenge" button. The audit reveals a CRITICAL
reliability problem: the forceAI pipeline fails to deliver AI-generated scenarios
69% of the time, silently falling back to handcrafted "Practice" scenarios. When
AI scenarios DO generate successfully, quality is generally good — score
perspectives are correct, situations are realistic, and Brain data integration
works well. However, coach lines are overwhelmingly generic, live generation
takes 30-70+ seconds, and there is no scenario deduplication.

OVERALL GRADE: C+
- AI Delivery Reliability: F (69% fallback rate)
- AI Scenario Quality (when delivered): B+
- Score Perspective Accuracy: A (no errors found)
- Coach Line Quality: D (mostly generic/streak-based)
- Loading Performance: D (live gen 30-70s, frequent timeouts)
- Explanation Quality: B (one weak AI explanation)
- Fallback Handling: C (silent, no user notification, wrong difficulty)

TOP 3 PRIORITIES:
1. Fix forceAI pipeline — 69% fallback rate makes AI Coach's Challenge broken
2. Overhaul coach line system — too many generic streak lines, duplicates, context mismatches
3. Add scenario deduplication — same scenario served twice in a row

6. TIMEOUT / FALLBACK ANALYSIS
================================================================================

FINDING: The fallback system works (no crashes or broken states), but it's
too aggressive and provides a poor user experience.

TIMEOUT BEHAVIOR OBSERVED:
- ThirdBase: ~60 seconds of "TAKING A LITTLE LONGER..." then fell back to
  a 1-star Rookie difficulty handcrafted scenario. The difficulty mismatch
  is a problem — AI Coach's Challenge should never serve Rookie difficulty.
- CenterField: ~70 seconds of loading, but DID eventually deliver an AI
  scenario (just barely within the 90-second AI_BUDGET).

FALLBACK QUALITY ISSUES:
1. NO USER NOTIFICATION: When forceAI falls back to handcrafted, the user
   sees a "Practice" badge but no message like "AI scenario unavailable,
   here's a challenging handcrafted one instead."
2. WRONG DIFFICULTY: ThirdBase fallback served a 1-star Rookie scenario
   via AI Coach's Challenge. Fallbacks should be filtered to diff >= 2.
3. NO RETRY OPTION: After fallback, no way to try AI generation again
   without returning to the home screen.

LOADING UX:
- "TAKING A LITTLE LONGER..." message appears after initial timeout
- No progress indicator or estimated wait time
- No cancel button to skip to a handcrafted scenario
- Wait times of 30-70 seconds are too long for a kids' app (target: 6-18)

FALLBACK RATE BY CAUSE:
- Pool empty (no pre-cached scenarios): ~6 of 8 fallbacks
- Timeout after live generation attempt: ~1 of 8 (ThirdBase)
- Unknown/immediate fallback: ~1 of 8

GRADE: C

7. EXPLANATION QUALITY
================================================================================

FINDING: Explanations are generally good for handcrafted scenarios and
adequate for AI scenarios, with one notable weakness.

AI SCENARIO EXPLANATIONS:
- Pitcher #1 (Wild Pitch Response): Good — explained why covering home plate
  is the pitcher's job on a wild pitch.
- Catcher #1 (Lead-Off Strike Strategy): WEAK — discussed wrong answer option
  instead of explaining why chosen correct answer was best. The explanation
  argued for "throw a fastball" but the correct answer was about pitch
  sequencing strategy.
- Batter #2 (Trailing By One): Good — explained count leverage and approach.
  Brain Insights integrated well.
- Shortstop #1 (Double Play Pivot): WEAK — only 1 sentence long. AI scenarios
  should provide at least 2-3 sentences explaining the strategic reasoning.
- CenterField #1 (Double Off Throw): Not tested (answer not submitted).

HANDCRAFTED SCENARIO EXPLANATIONS:
Generally strong across all tested positions. Multi-sentence, specific to the
game situation, and educational.

EXPLANATION ISSUES:
1. Catcher AI explanation discussed wrong option — suggests the AI prompt's
   explanation generation isn't consistently keyed to the correct answer.
2. Shortstop AI explanation was 1 sentence — too thin for educational value.
3. AI explanations don't consistently include the "why" — they sometimes
   state WHAT to do without explaining WHY it matters strategically.

GRADE: B

8. LOADING PERFORMANCE
================================================================================

PERFORMANCE BY SOURCE TYPE:
- Server pool (pre-fetched): ~3 seconds — EXCELLENT
- Local pool (browser-cached): ~1 second — EXCELLENT
- Handcrafted fallback: <1-5 seconds — GOOD
- Live AI generation (success): 30-70 seconds — POOR
- Live AI generation (timeout): 60+ seconds then fallback — VERY POOR

TARGET PERFORMANCE FOR KIDS APP:
- Ideal: < 3 seconds
- Acceptable: < 10 seconds
- Poor: 10-30 seconds
- Unacceptable: > 30 seconds

The pre-caching system (server pool + local pool) provides excellent
performance when pools are populated. The core problem is that pools are
frequently empty, forcing expensive live generation.

POOL AVAILABILITY DURING TESTING:
- Pitcher: Server pool had 1 scenario, then empty
- Catcher: Local pool had 1 scenario, then empty
- Batter: Pool empty — hit live generation or fallback
- Baserunner: Pool empty
- Manager: Pool empty
- FirstBase: Pool empty
- Shortstop: Pool empty — live generated in ~30s
- ThirdBase: Pool empty — live generation timed out
- CenterField: Pool empty — live generated in ~70s

Only 2 of 9 positions had ANY pool scenarios available. This suggests the
pre-caching system is either not running reliably or the pool is draining
faster than it refills.

GRADE: D

9. RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

PRIORITY 1 — CRITICAL (Fix within 1 week)
─────────────────────────────────────────

1.1 FIX forceAI PIPELINE RELIABILITY
    The AI Coach's Challenge button promises AI scenarios but delivers
    handcrafted 69% of the time. Root cause investigation needed:
    - Is the pre-cache system running? Pools were empty for 7 of 9 positions
    - Is live generation timing out? 2 of 3 live attempts took 30-70 seconds
    - Is there a race condition between pool check and fallback?
    ACTION: Add console logging at every decision point in the forceAI path.
    Instrument: pool check → pool miss → live gen start → live gen result →
    fallback trigger. Log WHY each fallback happens.

1.2 ADD SCENARIO DEDUPLICATION
    Baserunner was served the exact same scenario ("Steal Decision — Bad
    Count Changes Everything") twice in a row. The completed scenario ID
    list (state.cl) should be checked before serving ANY scenario, including
    fallbacks.
    ACTION: Add cl-check before fallback scenario selection.

1.3 FILTER FALLBACK DIFFICULTY
    When forceAI falls back to handcrafted, it served a 1-star Rookie
    scenario for ThirdBase. AI Coach's Challenge fallbacks should be
    filtered to difficulty >= 2.
    ACTION: Add `diff >= 2` filter in fallback scenario selection for
    forceAI=true calls.


PRIORITY 2 — HIGH (Fix within 2 weeks)
───────────────────────────────────────

2.1 OVERHAUL COACH LINE SYSTEM
    Current issues: 33% generic streak counters, 17% duplicates, context
    mismatches. Coach lines need three fixes:
    a) Position-awareness: Lines should reference the position's specific
       role (e.g., "Great cutoff throw!" for infielders, not "Stay locked in!")
    b) Scenario-awareness: Lines should reference the game situation or
       concept being tested
    c) Deduplication: Same line should not appear across different positions
       in the same session
    ACTION: Refactor getCoachLine() to accept position + concept parameters
    and select from position-specific line pools.

2.2 ADD FALLBACK USER NOTIFICATION
    When AI generation fails and a handcrafted scenario is served, show a
    brief toast: "AI scenario loading... here's a challenge while we prepare
    your custom scenario!" This manages expectations and explains the
    Practice badge.
    ACTION: Add toast notification in the fallback path of startGame().

2.3 IMPROVE AI EXPLANATION CONSISTENCY
    Two issues found: (a) Catcher explanation discussed wrong option,
    (b) Shortstop explanation was only 1 sentence.
    ACTION: Add post-generation validation that checks:
    - Explanation references the correct answer option specifically
    - Explanation is at least 2 sentences / 40 words
    - Explanation includes strategic reasoning (the "why")


PRIORITY 3 — MEDIUM (Fix within 1 month)
─────────────────────────────────────────

3.1 IMPROVE PRE-CACHE RELIABILITY
    Only 2 of 9 positions had pool scenarios. The pre-cache system should
    maintain at least 1-2 scenarios per position at all times.
    ACTION: Investigate pool fill rate, add monitoring for pool depth per
    position, consider eager pre-fetching on app load for popular positions.

3.2 ADD LOADING CANCEL BUTTON
    30-70 second waits are unacceptable for ages 6-18. Add a "Skip to
    Practice Scenario" button that appears after 10 seconds of loading.
    ACTION: Add cancel/skip button to the loading screen after 10s delay.

3.3 ADD "SOMETHING OFF?" FEEDBACK FOR ALL AI SCENARIOS
    The "Something off?" button was observed on the Shortstop AI scenario.
    Verify it appears consistently on ALL AI-generated scenarios and that
    the feedback is logged/actionable.
    ACTION: Audit feedback button rendering logic, verify D1 logging.

3.4 FIX CATCHER PERSPECTIVE BREAK
    Catcher AI scenario used "What should the catcher do?" instead of
    "What should you do?" — breaks the 2nd-person immersive perspective
    used throughout the app.
    ACTION: Add perspective validation to AI generation prompt and
    post-generation checks.

APPENDIX B: KNOWN LIMITATIONS OF THIS AUDIT
================================================================================

1. Testing was conducted via automated browser control (Claude in Chrome),
   which may have slightly different timing characteristics than manual use.

2. Only 13 scenarios were tested across 9 positions (target was 18). Some
   positions only got 1 scenario due to slow live generation consuming
   session time.

3. Console logs were not consistently captured for every scenario due to
   browser extension reconnection issues during long waits.

4. The CenterField #1 scenario loaded but the answer was not submitted
   before the session ended, so its explanation and coach line were not
   evaluated.

5. AI scenario quality may vary by time of day due to xAI API load.

6. The audit focused on the "AI Coach's Challenge" flow. Other AI entry
   points (e.g., exhausted scenarios triggering AI) were not tested.

7. Score perspective testing was limited to scenarios that were actually
   delivered. A comprehensive score audit would require testing the AI
   generation prompt with adversarial score setups.
