WHAT IS IT?

An interactive app that teaches baseball strategy through real game situations. Players pick a position, face a realistic scenario, choose from 4 options, and get immediate feedback explaining why their choice was right or wrong — with real MLB data behind every explanation.

WHAT YOUR PLAYERS LEARN

Cutoff & Relay Double Plays Hit & Run Pitch Sequencing Baserunning Reads Defensive Positioning Game Management Situational Hitting Bunt Defense Win Probability Rules & Plays Count Strategy

WHY COACHES LOVE IT

Team Code System

Create a team code and share it with your players. Track who's practicing, see your team's weakest concepts, and get a weekly report — all without anyone needing to create an account.

Age-Appropriate Content

Scenarios adapt to age groups (6-8, 9-10, 11-12, 13-15, 16-18). Younger players see simpler language and easier situations. Older players get advanced analytics and complex game theory.

Between-Practice Learning

Players can train their baseball IQ for 5 minutes a day on their phone. No equipment needed. Daily streaks keep them coming back. Speed rounds and survival mode make it competitive.
