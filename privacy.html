Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Baseball Strategy Master ("the App") is an educational baseball strategy game for players ages 6–18. We take privacy seriously, especially for young players. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Who We Are

Baseball Strategy Master is operated by Blaine LaFleur. You can reach us at blafleur@vested.marketing for any privacy questions.

What Data We Collect

Game progress data — Your score, level, completed scenarios, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to create an account in the future.

Anonymous analytics — We collect anonymized usage events (like "session started" or "scenario completed") to improve the game. These events contain no names, emails, or personally identifiable information. Analytics are processed via our Cloudflare Worker and stored in a Cloudflare D1 database.

AI-generated scenarios — When Pro users request AI coaching scenarios, we send your current level, position accuracy, and mastered concepts to xAI's API to generate personalized scenarios. No names or personal information are included in these requests.

Payment information — If you subscribe to the All-Star Pass (Pro), payment is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or process your credit card number. Stripe may store your email address for receipt delivery. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Error logs — If the app encounters an error, an anonymized error report may be sent to help us fix bugs. These contain no personal information.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers during normal gameplay
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not display third-party advertisements
  • We do not use location tracking

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is designed for children ages 6–18. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Game progress is stored locally on the device, not on our servers
  • The Pro upgrade is protected by a parent gate (math verification problem) to ensure a parent or guardian is present
  • Purchasing the All-Star Pass requires a credit card, which serves as verifiable parental consent under COPPA

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at blafleur@vested.marketing and we will delete it.

Data Storage and Security

Game data is stored locally in your browser. Anonymous analytics and error logs are stored in Cloudflare's infrastructure (D1 database), which provides encryption at rest. Our Cloudflare Worker API uses HTTPS for all communications and rate limiting to prevent abuse.

Your Rights

You can clear all your game data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site. If you need us to delete any server-side data associated with your account, contact us at blafleur@vested.marketing.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare — Hosting and API infrastructure (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (privacy policy)
  • xAI — AI scenario generation for Pro users (privacy policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Significant changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact
